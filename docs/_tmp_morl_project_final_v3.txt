                                             Abstract
         Four phases of research toward MORL-based HVAC control. Phase 0: PPO baseline
     (28% energy reduction). Phase 1: RC Neural ODE surrogate (32,313× speedup, RMSE
     0.163°C). Phase 2: inverse problem calibration (79% RMSE improvement). Phase 3: ten
     systematic experiments culminating in a three-layer architecture—HDRL with real weather,
     5-feature observations (Tamb -aware), expanded action space [18, 30]°C, and Emergency Heat-
     ing Mode—achieving ms = 0.704 ± 0.369 across 12 monthly scenarios (168 days, ≈1,142
     years), with summer ms = 0.140–0.173. BOPTEST fine-tuning confirmed the remaining gap
     is structural (clip_fraction = 0). 15 problems documented.
